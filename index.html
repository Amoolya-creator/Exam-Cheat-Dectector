Examination Cheat Detector

This Program is designed to catch cheats in examination hall with MCQ-type of papers.

The program displays sets of Roll Numbers of students who have high incidence of similar answers.

Since brilliant students would score highly and their answers would be matching too, hence this program also picks those students. The decision whether a set of students is a brilliant cohort or a cheat group, can be deduced by the percentage of correct replies from them. A high cut-off of 90% should be sufficient to separate the two groups. However this factor is arbitrary and can be modified to suit individual needs. One can adjust the Cut-off Factor in the program.

This program is a good tool for Administration / Universities / Councils / Boards to implement and minimise cheating practices. They can state in Terms & Conditions of their examinations that groups of students who shall be flagged by this program may have to appear in a separate "distinctive" examination to prove their calibre. This step shall identify any cheats.
Disclaimer: This is a trial of how technology can be used to curtail cheating and catch cheats, please use at your own risk and discretion.
